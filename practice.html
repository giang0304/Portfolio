Project Overview


Risaiguma is a food waste recycle service, in which we collect food waste from households and compost this waste into organic fertilizers for farmers.
Our service motivates food waste separation through the use of produce coupons at supermarkets, as our reward for efforts to reduce huge food waste loss daily.
Supported with our specialized software application, food waste separation is easier than ever with simple guidance of separation method, collection days, and coupons information.



Background



delete_forever
Tons of food wasted

Japan is suffering from 6.5 millions tons of food waste each year, while only 0.7 millions tons is properly recycled. The rest of food waste goes straight to landfills, which wastes land resources and pollute the air from waste burning.


error_outline
No habit of recycling food waste yet

Especially urban residences who do not have time for further separation of waste.


sync_problem
Complicated food waste separation process

Due to several laid rules of food waste separation for organic recycling purposes, separation becomes more difficult, especially to people new to garbage separation etiquette.



Our Solution


emoji_objects Risaiguma is a service that supports food waste separation habit which helps:

1. Simplify food waste separation steps

2. Motivate the good habit of waste separation

3. Support local farmers with organic compost from food waste collected





Users Insights



person_pin
“I don’t know how to properly do Food Waste separation.”

The app should have an easy flow for beginners, and easy guidance with friendly interface and useful information.


person_pin
“It is too dificult to separate a whole new category of waste!”

With our app, we help with simple guidance for food waste separation by waste scanner, collection calendar, and checklist. We also motivate users by coupons reward for cheaper and organic produce.



person_pin
“I do not have time because I am busy.”

Our service will collect the waste at the front porch of your house on designated days, which will be notified through the application.


person_pin
“This model has no profit!”

We are offering our organic compost to farmers free of charge. However, we will receive a small percentage from farmer’s profit. In addition, we also sell the organic compost to chain retailers.
